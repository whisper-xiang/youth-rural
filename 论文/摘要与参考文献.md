# 基于微信小程序的大学生暑期"三下乡"活动管理系统的设计与实现

---

## 摘要

随着高校社会实践活动的深入开展，大学生暑期"三下乡"活动已成为高校育人工作的重要组成部分。然而，传统的活动管理方式存在信息传递不畅、审批流程繁琐、过程监管困难等问题，难以满足现代化管理需求。本文针对上述问题，设计并实现了一套基于微信小程序的大学生暑期"三下乡"活动管理系统。

本系统采用前后端分离的架构模式，前端基于微信小程序原生框架开发，后端采用Node.js + Express框架构建RESTful API服务，数据存储使用MySQL关系型数据库。系统实现了项目申报、多级审批、进度跟踪、成果管理、评优评审等核心功能模块，支持学生、指导教师、学院管理员、校级管理员、评审专家五种角色的差异化操作。

通过系统测试验证，本系统功能完整、运行稳定、操作便捷，能够有效提升"三下乡"活动的管理效率和信息化水平，为高校社会实践活动管理提供了可行的技术解决方案。

**关键词**：微信小程序；三下乡；活动管理系统；Node.js；MySQL

---

## Abstract

With the in-depth development of social practice activities in colleges and universities, the summer "Going to the Countryside" activity for college students has become an important part of the educational work in universities. However, traditional activity management methods have problems such as poor information transmission, cumbersome approval processes, and difficult process supervision, which are difficult to meet the needs of modern management. This paper designs and implements a management system for college students' summer "Going to the Countryside" activities based on WeChat Mini Program to address the above problems.

The system adopts a front-end and back-end separation architecture. The front-end is developed based on the WeChat Mini Program native framework, the back-end uses Node.js + Express framework to build RESTful API services, and MySQL relational database is used for data storage. The system implements core functional modules such as project application, multi-level approval, progress tracking, achievement management, and excellence evaluation, supporting differentiated operations for five roles: students, supervisors, college administrators, school-level administrators, and review experts.

Through system testing and verification, this system has complete functions, stable operation, and convenient operation. It can effectively improve the management efficiency and informatization level of "Going to the Countryside" activities, and provides a feasible technical solution for the management of social practice activities in colleges and universities.

**Keywords**: WeChat Mini Program; Going to the Countryside; Activity Management System; Node.js; MySQL
